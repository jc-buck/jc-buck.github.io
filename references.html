REFERENCES

References for students

I'm happy to provide references for any of the ~400 students I've taught at Durham. If you're a previous student who would like a reference, please email me with the following information.
  • Name (legal and preferred, plus pronouns if desired)
  • Year(s) in which I taught you
  • Module(s) for which I taught you
  • The position for which you would like the reference
  • The date by which the reference would be needed*
  • Your academic transcript (or as recent a record of marks as possible)
  • Additional relevant experience

* Please note that I require a minimum of 1 month's notice to produce a reference. If you require the reference sooner, I may not be able to provide one.
