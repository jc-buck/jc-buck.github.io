BIOGRAPHY

General information

I'm in the final year of my PhD in law at Durham University, where I've previously completed my LLB and MJur degrees. My PhD is funded by the Arts and Humanities Research Council (AHRC) through the Northern Bridge Consortium, and I'm very fortunate to be supervised by Dr Sam Halliday and Prof Emma Cave.

I've been teaching at Durham Law School since 2021, and I've been Senior Tutor in Law since 2022. Previously, I co-convened the Centre for Ethics and Law in the Life Sciences (CELLS), was a research and teaching assistant on two LLM modules, and was one of two course representatives for postgraduate research students.

I've been involved in university strategy, recruitment, and relations. I currently sit on the editorial board of the Philosophy, Public Policy, and Transnational Law book series.
