BIOGRAPHY

General information

I recently passed my PhD with no corrections at Durham University, where I've previously completed my LLB and MJur degrees. My PhD was funded by the Arts and Humanities Research Council (AHRC) through the Northern Bridge Consortium, and I'm very fortunate to have been supervised by Dr Sam Halliday and Prof Emma Cave.

I taught at Durham Law School from 2021 to 2025, and I was Senior Tutor in Law from 2022 to 2025. I co-convened the Centre for Ethics and Law in the Life Sciences (CELLS), was a research and teaching assistant on two LLM modules, and was one of two course representatives for postgraduate research students.

I was involved in university strategy, recruitment, and relations. I currently sit on the editorial board of the Philosophy, Public Policy, and Transnational Law book series.
